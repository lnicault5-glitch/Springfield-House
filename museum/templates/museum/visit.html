Plan Your Visit

Discover the heritage, stories, and nature surrounding Springfield House. Whether you stop by spontaneously or book a guided tour, your visit supports the preservation of this historic estate and its community legacy.

📍 Location

Springfield Estate, between Pont Cassé and Canefield, Dominica
Easily accessible along the main road from Roseau to the international airport. View on Google Maps →

🕓 Opening Hours

Monday – Wednesday: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
Friday – Saturday: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
Thursday & Sunday: Closed

💚 Entry & Support

Springfield House operates through visitor support and community contributions.
Entry: EC$40 per adult
Children (under 15): Free admission
Resident discounts
All contributions help fund restoration, storytelling, and community programs.

🌿 What to Expect

  • Walk through the museum and nature trail
  • Archival photos and local stories
  • Art displays and rotating exhibits
  • Rest areas with mountain and sea views
