Springfield Forward

Preserving heritage, protecting biodiversity, and pioneering new ways to connect people with place — Springfield House is evolving into a 21st-century ecosystem where culture, science, and AI meet.

Our Mission

Every contribution helps sustain a living legacy. The restoration of Springfield House is not only about preserving walls and gardens — it is about nurturing ideas, knowledge, and species. Your support strengthens our ability to protect, research, and share Dominica’s natural and cultural wealth with the world.

🪴 Sustainable Restoration

Restoring heritage architecture and garden landscapes using environmentally responsible practices. Every donation supports the long-term preservation of Springfield’s history.

🌿 Biodiversity & Research

Supporting plant propagation, forest regeneration, and ongoing ecological research. This ensures the survival of local and endangered species.

🎨 Cultural Enhancement

Documenting oral histories, local craftsmanship, and artistic expression through digital exhibits, publications, and community workshops.

🤖 Digital Innovation & AI

Building interactive archives, QR-based storytelling, and virtual exhibits using modern technology and AI-driven creativity.
